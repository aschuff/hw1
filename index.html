Me Before

Hi, my name is Amanda. I'm from a small town called Pelzer located in Greenville, SC. I grew up with two brothers and lots of animals. I graduated with my degree in Art Education from the University of South Carolina in 2012.

Along the Way

Since 2013 I have been teaching elementary Art to kids from Kindergarten all the way up to 5th grade. Elementary was never my first choice (I really wanted to teach high school) but I saw it as an opportunity and a challenge so I went for it.

I was excited and had this feeling that I could conquer anthing that was put in front of me. However, after a few months Year One started to feel a little like being in a boat with a pinhole in the bottom; you just keep shoveling out the water just to stay afloat. As the year went on I kept my head up and stayed positive. Summer came and went and before I knew it I wasn't a "baby" teacher anymore. I was on to Year Two and my hopes were high! And then reality set in once again. Anyway, long story short, after 3 solid years I finally accepted that maybe it just wasn't for me.
